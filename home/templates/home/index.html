    About

    The Sports & Games Council of the IIT (BHU) Gymkhana offers all its future IIT’ians inclined the chance to explore the world that lay outside books. Featuring over a wide range of games and sports, students can play the sport of their choice. The council is the abode of a vibrant community of passionate sportspersons among whom many have won recognition at the national level. With our fully equipped courts and experienced coaches even a beginner can gain expertise in a sport of their choice. Regular practice and various events are the secret behind our achievements. Teams from the council have won laurels in the inter-IIT meets, and the council’s annual sports fest SPARDHA is the largest in North India. Beyond just sports, the council is instrumental in instilling discipline and sportsmanship in the students.
